Page B — AI Agents (Engineering Perspective)

From an engineering standpoint, AI agents must be reliable, observable, and controllable. Production agents require logging, versioning, and rollback capabilities to ensure safe operation.

Key challenges include preventing infinite loops, controlling API costs, and ensuring that memory systems do not introduce stale or conflicting information.
