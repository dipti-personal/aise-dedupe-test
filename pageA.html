Page A — AI Agents

AI agents are autonomous software entities that perceive their environment, make decisions, and perform actions to achieve defined goals. Modern AI agents combine large language models, planning logic, and external tools to solve complex problems.

In real-world systems, AI agents are used for customer support, coding assistance, workflow automation, and research tasks. They often rely on memory stores, retrieval systems, and decision policies to determine their next action.
